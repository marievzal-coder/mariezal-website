The artist · 01

Maryia
Zaloznaya

Born in Belarus and now based in Los Angeles, Maryia began drawing in early childhood. After formal art school, she chose to unlearn convention and paint from lived experience — translating pain, transformation and human connection into symbolic, emotionally direct works.

Her paintings have travelled with her through Minsk, Sochi, Warsaw, Barcelona, Tenerife and Los Angeles. Each place, encounter and rupture enters the work.

“My paintings are my path, my vision of the world and my strength.”
