The practice · Alchemy of experience

What wounded us
can become
radiant.

Maryia Zaloznaya transforms pain into image, memory into symbol and survival into beauty. Shaped by a life lived across countries and cultures, her work speaks to the hidden self --- the part of us that longs to be witnessed, reclaimed and set free.

Meet the artist

The hand remembers

Every layer
leaves a trace.

Maryia works where the body, memory and material meet. Paint gathers on the hand as experience gathers on a life: imperfectly, intimately, layer by layer. Each work carries evidence of touch --- the pressure, hesitation and resolve that no reproduction can repeat.
