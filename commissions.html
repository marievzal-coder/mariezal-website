The process

  1. 01

    Conversation

    Share the story, feeling or turning point you want the work to carry, alongside scale, palette and meaningful symbols.

  2. 02

    Concept

    Maryia distils the conversation into an emotional direction and a private visual concept created only for you.

  3. 03

    Creation

    Your one-of-one work is brought to life in Los Angeles, with thoughtful updates at key moments of its transformation.

  4. 04

    Placement

    The finished work is signed, certified and prepared with care for its place in your life --- locally or anywhere in the world.
